KRISTINE TOMLINSON


CASI Level 2 and Snow Park Certified Instructor

ABOUT

I'm a 24-year-old girl with a passion for snowboarding and wakeboarding. I have been doing both for about 8 years now and I don't think I'll be stopping any time soon. I love teaching and sharing my love for the sports with others. I find it incredibly rewarding to help others reach their riding goals. I'm a hard-working, enthusiastic and out-going individual. I am self-motivated and always working towards progressing my skills as both a rider and instructor.

Safety is the number one priority during my lessons, because you can't have fun when you're hurt. I pick terrain best suited for the students, and teach new tricks in many steps of progression so that they are never trying anything out of their ability level.

My second priority is fun!!! I adapt my lessons to best accommodate the students. Some students enjoy a lot of verbal explanation while others just want to get out and try things. In either case, I try to organize the lessons so that the student is continuing to develop their skills and having fun while doing it.

EXPERIENCE

Fernie Alpine Resort


2010/2011: Worked full time as a snowboard instructor for the 2010/2011 season. By working full time I gained experience teaching all different ages groups (ages 4 to 50 and above) and sizes of groups (private one-on-ones to school groups of about 15 students). I won an award for having the second highest amount of snowboard lesson sales in the month of February. I feel that this demonstrates my likability as an instructor as my students would always request to have me as their instructor after having me once.

2011/2012: Fernie Alpine Resort was gracious enough to hire me for just the busy times of the year (Christmas and Family Weekend), so that I was able to instruct over my breaks from University.

2012/2013: I helped out with the Burton Girls Snowboard Clinic, where I worked with a group of about 7 women to help them progress their freestyle skills over the course of a weekend.

Wapiti Valley


2012/2013: Volunteered as Wapiti Valley's snowboard instructor. I mostly worked with children aged 8 to 14, and ran two lessons a night, twice a week.
